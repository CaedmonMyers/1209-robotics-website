Java the Hutt


Java the Hutt Scouting is our new scouting app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It's available to download for free from the App Store for all FRC teams.


About

Java the Hutt Scouting provides a convinient way to collect scouting data for your team. It is designed to work offline on your device when you don't have a wifi conection, then sync online when your phone is connected.
